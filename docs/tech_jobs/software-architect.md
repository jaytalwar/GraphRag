## Job Description
A **Software Architect** is a professional responsible for designing the overall structure and high-level architecture of software systems. Makes critical technical decisions and ensures systems are scalable, maintainable, and aligned with business goals.

## Key Responsibilities
- Design high-level software architecture and system components
- Make technical decisions regarding technologies, frameworks, and patterns
- Ensure architectural compliance and maintain technical standards
- Collaborate with stakeholders to understand requirements and constraints
- Mentor development teams on architectural principles and best practices

## Required Skills
- **Technical Skills:**
  - **Architecture Patterns:** Microservices, SOA, Event-driven, Layered architecture
  - **Programming:** Multiple languages (Java, Python, C#, JavaScript)
  - **Databases:** SQL, NoSQL, database design principles
  - **Cloud Platforms:** AWS, Azure, GCP, containerization
  - **Tools:** UML, architecture modeling tools, documentation platforms

- **Soft Skills:**
  - Strategic thinking
  - Leadership and mentoring
  - Communication and presentation
  - Decision-making
  - Problem-solving
