## Job Description
An **Stand-up Comedian** is a professional Performing artist who entertains audiences through live comedy performances, storytelling, and humor.

## Key Responsibilities
- Develop and perform original comedy material
- Perform at comedy clubs, theaters, and events
- Write and refine comedic scripts and routines
- Engage with audiences during live performances
- Promote shows and build fan following

## Required Skills
- **Technical Skills:**
  - Microphone technique and stage presence
  - Comedy writing and timing
  - Audience interaction skills
  - Social media marketing
  - Performance booking and logistics
- **Soft Skills:**
  - Humor and wit
  - Confidence
  - Resilience
  - Observational skills
  - Improvisation

## Related Careers
- Comedy Writer
- Actor
- Entertainer
- Voice-over Artist

## Salary Range
$40,000 per year

## Education Requirements
No formal requirements; comedy workshops and performance training helpful

Comedy & Performance
