# Radiologist

## Job Description
A *Radiologist* is a medical doctor who specializes in diagnosing and treating diseases and injuries using medical imaging techniques such as X-rays, computed tomography (CT), magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), and ultrasound.

## Key Responsibilities
- Interpreting medical images to diagnose conditions.
- Consulting with other physicians to help determine the best course of treatment.
- Performing interventional procedures guided by imaging, such as biopsies or tumor ablations.
- Ensuring the safety of patients and staff during imaging procedures.
- Overseeing the quality of images produced by radiologic technologists.

## Required Skills
- *Technical Skills:*
    - *Image Interpretation:* Expertise in analyzing various types of medical scans.
    - *Medical Imaging Technology:* Deep understanding of how MRI, CT, and X-ray machines work.
    - *Anatomy:* Detailed knowledge of human anatomy as seen on scans.
    - *PACS/RIS:* Proficiency with Picture Archiving and Communication Systems.
- *Soft Skills:*
    - Visual acuity and attention to detail
    - Analytical and problem-solving skills
    - Written communication for reports
    - Collaboration with other specialists
